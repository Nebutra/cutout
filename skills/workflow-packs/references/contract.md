# Contract

- Protocol: `cutout.workflow-pack.v1`.
- Storage: `.cutout/workflows/*.json` below the controlled project root.
- Packs are versioned, provenance-bound DAG manifests.
- Effects and approvals are declarative and remain subject to Cutout policy.
- Eval cards require explicit metric evidence and do not invoke a model.
- External Coding Agents remain external controllers through CLI/MCP.
